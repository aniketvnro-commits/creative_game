Game context
Before you start Think like a system designer
You are not just drawing — you are designing a living system.

Each team will create a creature that must survive in a specific environment using only the shapes given.

This is not about drawing skills. This is about how you think under constraints.

Rule 01
Visually use the shapes you received
Rule 02
Function logically — its body should justify its abilities
Rule 03
Fit the mission world it belongs to
At the end, you are not judged on beauty — you are judged on thinking, storytelling, and logic under limitation.

How it plays
Round 1
Build Your Creature
10 min
Using only the 3 shapes drawn, create your creature on paper. Every shape must appear at least once. You can repeat, overlap, rotate — but no new shapes allowed.
Round 2
Name + Powers
5 min
Give your creature: a name, one superpower, and something it eats. These must connect logically to how it looks and the world it survives in.
Round 3
Creature Parade
10 min
Stick all drawings on the wall. Each team gets 60 seconds to introduce their creature dramatically. Audience asks one question per creature. Then voting begins.
Voting
Pick Your Winners
5 min
Vote across 4 categories — one winner each. A creature can win multiple awards. Clap voting only, no deliberation. Facilitator tallies points live on the scoreboard.
